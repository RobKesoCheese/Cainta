A Legacy Beyond Measure

Step into a town where history lives in every corner. Founded in 1707, Cainta is one of the oldest and most culturally unique settlements in Rizal. Our story is defined by the resilience of the Sepoy soldiers and our pivotal role in the Philippine Revolution. Today, this heritage continues to thrive through our vibrant traditions and the warm hospitality of our people.
