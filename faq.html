General Information & Operations

1. What are the operating hours of the Cainta Municipal Hall? +
The Cainta Municipal Hall is open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, excluding regular national holidays and declared local holidays.
2. Where is the Cainta Municipal Hall located? +
It is located at A. Bonifacio Ave., Barangay Sto. Domingo, Cainta, Rizal.
3. Who is the current Mayor of Cainta? +
The current Mayor of Cainta is Atty. J. Keith "Kit" P. Nieto.
Read the Mayor's Profile.
4. Who is the current Vice Mayor? +
The current Vice Mayor is Hon. Ace Bernardo Servillon.
Read the Vice Mayor's Profile.
5. Who are the Sangguniang Bayan Members? +
Our municipality has 8 distinguished Sangguniang Bayan members who shape local legislation.
View all SB Members here.

Business Permits & Taxes

6. How do I apply for a new Business Permit? +
You can apply at the Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO). Requirements: Barangay Clearance, DTI/SEC Registration, and Contract of Lease (if renting).
View BPLO Office.
7. When is the deadline for Business Permit Renewal? +
Business permit renewals are typically conducted every January 1 to 20 of the year.
8. What are the requirements for a Working/Occupational Permit? +
Applicants must secure a Health Certificate from the Municipal Health Office, a Police Clearance, and a Barangay Clearance before proceeding to the BPLO for the Mayor's Permit to Work.
9. Can I pay my Real Property Taxes (RPT) online? +
Yes, to avoid long lines, we highly encourage residents to use the Cainta E-Payment Portal for RPT and Business Tax assessments.
10. Where do I pay my taxes physically? +
Physical tax payments and fee collections are handled by the Municipal Treasury Office located on the Ground Floor of the Municipal Hall.
View Treasury Office.

Health, Hospital & Emergency

11. Who do I call for medical emergencies or rescue? +
For immediate response, contact the Cainta MDRRMO (RESCUE 131) at 8535-0131. They are operational 24/7.
View all Hotlines.
12. What is the hotline for the Cainta Police? +
For police assistance, you can contact the Cainta PNP or coordinate with the Municipal Public Safety Office.
View Public Safety Office.
13. What is the hotline for the Fire Station? +
For fire emergencies, please call the local Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) at 8696-2616.
14. Does Cainta have a public hospital? +
Yes! The Cainta Municipal Hospital offers extensive medical services, out-patient checkups, and emergency care.
View Hospital Services.
15. Where can I get a COVID-19 vaccination or test? +
Vaccinations are distributed across our Rural Health Units (RHUs). Antigen testing is available at Liwasang Bayan.
View COVID-19 Service Locations.
16. Where do I go for animal bite injections? +
You can visit the Municipal Health Office for anti-rabies vaccines, or the Veterinary Office for animal-related concerns.
View Veterinary Office.

Social Services & Identification

17. What are the requirements for a Senior Citizen ID? +
Visit the OSCA Office. Requirements: Birth Certificate or valid ID showing age (60+ years old) and Cainta address, 1x1 photo, and the application form.
View ID Requirements.
18. How do I get a PWD ID? +
Proceed to the PDAO Office. Requirements: Medical Certificate, valid Gov ID with Cainta address, two 2x2 photos, and the filled-out application form.
View PDAO Office.
19. How do I apply for a Solo Parent ID? +
Visit the MSWD Office. Requirements: Barangay Certification (6 mos residency), proof of solo parent status, Income Tax Return (ITR), birth certificates of children, and CENOMAR.
20. How can I ask for financial or medical assistance? +
You may seek assistance through the Malasakit Center or the Mayor's Action Team (MAT) located at the Municipal Hall.
View Malasakit Center.

Infrastructure, Transport & Environment

21. Where do I apply for a Building Permit? +
Building permits, occupancy permits, and structural assessments are handled by the Municipal Engineering Office.
View Engineering Office.
22. Who handles garbage collection schedules? +
The Waste Management Office oversees the daily collection schedules for all barangays in Cainta.
View Waste Management.
23. How do I register or renew my tricycle franchise? +
Tricycle franchising and TODA regulations are processed by the Cainta Tricycle Regulatory Unit (CTRU).
View CTRU Office.
24. How do I contact my Barangay Hall? +
We have a complete directory of the 7 Barangays of Cainta (San Andres, San Isidro, San Juan, San Roque, Sta. Rosa, Sto. Domingo, Sto. Niño) including their numbers and emails.
View Barangay Directory.

Education, Culture & Transparency

25. What courses are offered at One Cainta College? +
One Cainta College offers degrees in Technical Vocational Teacher Education, Entrepreneurship, and Information Systems, plus Diploma ladderized programs.
View College Info.
26. Does the municipality offer job placement help? +
Yes, the Public Employment Service Office (PESO) frequently hosts job fairs and assists residents with local and overseas employment.
View PESO Office.
27. What are the best tourist spots in Cainta? +
Popular landmarks include the Museo de Cainta, One Cainta Arena, Oval Park, ROTC Grounds, and Sta. Lucia Mall.
View Tourist Carousel.
28. What is the history of the Sepoys in Cainta? +
Indian soldiers (Sepoys) settled in Cainta after the Seven Years' War in 1762. Their descendants remain prominent today, deeply influencing our local culture and delicacies.
Read our Full History.
29. How can I get a copy of my Birth, Marriage, or Death Certificate? +
Request these civil registry documents at the Local Civil Registry Office (LCRO) on the First Floor. Bring a valid ID and pay the processing fee.
View LCRO Office.
30. Where can I see the municipality's financial reports? +
In compliance with DILG directives, all our annual budgets, procurement plans, and financial reports are publicly available for download.
View Full Disclosure Board.
