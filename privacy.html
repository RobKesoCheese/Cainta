Data Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

The Local Government Unit (LGU) of Cainta is committed to protecting and respecting your personal data privacy. We comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173). This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our portal and municipal services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, address, contact details, birth date, and government-issued identification numbers when you:

  • Apply for municipal permits, clearances, and licenses.
  • Avail of social services, health programs, and educational scholarships.
  • Submit complaints, inquiries, or feedback through our contact forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is utilized exclusively for public service delivery, including verifying identities for public documents, communicating updates regarding your transactions, improving municipal services, and fulfilling legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Data Protection and Security

We implement strict physical, technical, and organizational security measures to prevent unauthorized access, processing, or disclosure of your data. Only authorized personnel from the Municipality of Cainta have access to this information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your rights under the Data Privacy Act, please contact the Cainta Data Protection Officer via our Contact Us page.
